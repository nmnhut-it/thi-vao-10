# Unit 53 - Exercises: Verb + -ing

## 53.1 Complete the sentences for each situation. Use -ing.
1. 'What shall we do?' / 'We could go to the zoo.' → She suggested **going to the zoo.**
2. 'Do you want to play tennis?' / 'No, not really.' → He didn't fancy ___
3. 'Let's go for a walk.' / 'Good idea!' → She suggested ___
4. 'You caused the accident.' / 'No, I didn't.' → He denied ___
5. 'Can you wait a few minutes?' / 'No, I didn't.' → They didn't mind ___
6. 'You didn't tell the truth.' / 'That's right. I didn't.' → She admitted ___
