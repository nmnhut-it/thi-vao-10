# The Subjunctive (Thuc gia dinh)

Tai lieu ngu phap danh cho hoc sinh lop 9 on thi vao 10 THPT.

---

## 1. Present Subjunctive (Gia dinh hien tai)

### 1.1. Sau cac dong tu: suggest, recommend, insist, demand, propose, request

Khi muon dien dat loi de nghi, yeu cau, doi hoi, ta dung cau truc:

**S + suggest/recommend/insist/demand/propose/request + that + S + V (bare infinitive)**

Dong tu trong menh de "that" luon o dang nguyen mau (khong chia), bat ke chu ngu la gi.

**Vi du:**
- The teacher **suggested** that every student **study** harder. (khong phai "studies")
- Doctors **recommend** that she **drink** more water. (khong phai "drinks")
- He **insisted** that the meeting **be** held on Friday. (khong phai "is" hay "was")
- The principal **demanded** that the students **wear** uniforms.
- We **proposed** that the project **start** next month.

> **Luu y quan trong:** Voi "he/she/it", dong tu KHONG them "-s". Voi phu dinh, dung "not + V" (khong dung "don't"):
> - I suggested that he **not go** out late. (khong phai "doesn't go")

### 1.2. Sau tinh tu: important, vital, necessary, essential, crucial

**It is + important/essential/necessary/vital/crucial + that + S + V (bare infinitive)**

**Vi du:**
- It is **essential** that everyone **be** here on time.
- It is **important** that she **attend** the meeting.
- It is **necessary** that the report **be submitted** before Friday.

### 1.3. Cach dung thay the (pho bien hon trong giao tiep)

Trong tieng Anh hang ngay, nguoi ta thuong dung "should + V" thay cho subjunctive:

- I suggested that he **should study** harder. (= I suggested that he study harder.)
- It's essential that everyone **should be** here. (= It's essential that everyone be here.)

Ca hai cach deu dung. Trong bai thi, hay doc ky de bai de chon dung dang.

## 2. Past Subjunctive (Gia dinh qua khu)

Past subjunctive dung thi qua khu don (Past Simple) nhung **khong noi ve qua khu** ma noi ve dieu **khong co that o hien tai** hoac dieu **muon thay doi o hien tai**.

### 2.1. Wish / If only + Past Simple

Dien dat dieu uoc ve hien tai (dieu khong co that bay gio).

**S + wish / If only + S + V (Past Simple)**

**Vi du:**
- I **wish** I **knew** the answer. (Toi uoc gi toi biet cau tra loi -- nhung toi khong biet.)
- She **wishes** she **had** more free time. (Co ay uoc co nhieu thoi gian ranh hon -- nhung khong co.)
- **If only** I **were** taller! (Gia ma toi cao hon!)
- He **wishes** he **could** speak English fluently.

> **Luu y:** Voi dong tu "be", dung **"were"** cho tat ca cac ngoi (I were, he were, she were...). Trong giao tiep, "was" cung duoc chap nhan, nhung trong bai thi nen dung "were".

### 2.2. It's time / It's high time / It's about time + Past Simple

Dien dat viec ai do nen lam gi ngay bay gio (da den luc phai lam).

**It's (high/about) time + S + V (Past Simple)**

**Vi du:**
- It's time we **went** home. (Da den luc chung ta ve nha roi.)
- It's high time you **studied** seriously for the exam.
- It's about time he **found** a job. (Da den luc anh ay tim viec di.)
- It's time the children **were** in bed.

> **Phan biet:** "It's time **to go**" (den luc di) -- dung voi to-infinitive, chi noi chung. "It's time we **went**" -- nhan manh rang le ra phai lam tu lau roi, mang sac thai hoi trach moc.

### 2.3. Would rather + S + Past Simple

Dien dat mong muon nguoi khac lam gi.

**S + would rather + (that) + S + V (Past Simple)**

**Vi du:**
- I'd rather you **stayed** here tonight. (Toi muon ban o lai day toi nay.)
- She'd rather he **didn't come** to the party.
- Would you rather I **drove**? (Ban co muon toi lai xe khong?)

> **Phan biet:** "I'd rather **go**" = toi thich di hon (chinh minh lam). "I'd rather you **went**" = toi muon ban di (nguoi khac lam).

### 2.4. As if / As though + Past Simple

Dien dat dieu khong co that o hien tai, ai do cu lam nhu the la that.

**S + V (present) + as if / as though + S + V (Past Simple)**

**Vi du:**
- He talks **as if** he **knew** everything. (Anh ta noi chuyen nhu the biet het moi thu -- nhung khong phai.)
- She acts **as though** she **were** the boss.
- He spends money **as if** he **were** a millionaire.

## 3. Past Perfect Subjunctive (Gia dinh qua khu hoan thanh)

Dien dat dieu uoc ve qua khu (dieu da khong xay ra, bay gio tiec nuoi).

### 3.1. Wish / If only + Past Perfect (had + V3/ed)

**S + wish / If only + S + had + V3/ed**

**Vi du:**
- I **wish** I **had studied** harder last year. (Toi uoc gi nam ngoai toi da hoc cham chi hon.)
- She **wishes** she **hadn't said** those words.
- **If only** we **had known** about the exam earlier!
- He **wishes** he **had taken** that job offer.

### 3.2. As if / As though + Past Perfect

Dien dat dieu khong co that o qua khu.

- He looked **as if** he **had seen** a ghost. (Anh ta tron mat nhu the da nhin thay ma.)
- She talked about Paris **as though** she **had been** there. (Nhung co ay chua tung den.)

---

## 4. Bang tom tat cau truc

| Cau truc | Thoi dong tu | Y nghia |
|---|---|---|
| suggest/recommend/insist + that + S + V | Bare infinitive | De nghi, yeu cau |
| wish / if only + S + V | Past Simple | Dieu uoc hien tai |
| wish / if only + S + had + V3 | Past Perfect | Dieu uoc qua khu |
| it's time + S + V | Past Simple | Da den luc |
| would rather + S + V | Past Simple | Muon nguoi khac lam |
| as if / as though + S + V | Past Simple | Khong that o hien tai |
| as if / as though + S + had + V3 | Past Perfect | Khong that o qua khu |

## 5. Cac dang de thi thuong gap

### Dang 1: Viet lai cau (Sentence rewrite)

**De:** "I'm sorry I don't know the answer." -> Rewrite using "wish".
**Dap an:** I **wish** I **knew** the answer.

**De:** "She didn't study for the test." -> Rewrite using "If only".
**Dap an:** **If only** she **had studied** for the test.

**De:** "The doctor said: 'You should exercise every day.'" -> Rewrite using "recommend".
**Dap an:** The doctor **recommended** that I **exercise** every day.

### Dang 2: Chon dap an dung

**De:** It's high time you ______ (start) revising for the exam.
- A. start  B. started  C. starting  D. to start
**Dap an:** B. started

**De:** She wishes she ______ (can) play the piano.
- A. can  B. could  C. will  D. would
**Dap an:** B. could

### Dang 3: Chia dong tu

**De:** The manager insisted that the report (submit) ______ by Friday.
**Dap an:** be submitted

## 6. Loi sai thuong gap (Common Mistakes)

| Sai | Dung | Giai thich |
|---|---|---|
| I wish I **am** rich. | I wish I **were** rich. | Wish + Past Simple |
| If only I **didn't say** that yesterday. | If only I **hadn't said** that yesterday. | Qua khu -> Past Perfect |
| He suggested that she **goes** home. | He suggested that she **go** home. | Subjunctive: bare infinitive |
| It's time we **go** home. | It's time we **went** home. | It's time + Past Simple |
| I'd rather you **don't** call me. | I'd rather you **didn't** call me. | Would rather + S + Past Simple |
| She acts as if she **is** the queen. | She acts as if she **were** the queen. | As if + were (khong that) |

## 7. Bai tap tu luyen

**Chia dong tu trong ngoac:**

1. The teacher suggested that we (read) ______ the book carefully.
2. I wish I (have) ______ a bigger house.
3. It's high time the government (do) ______ something about pollution.
4. She'd rather you (not / mention) ______ it to anyone.
5. If only I (listen) ______ to my parents' advice last year.
6. He talks as if he (own) ______ the company.
7. It is essential that every student (attend) ______ the ceremony.
8. I wish it (not / rain) ______ right now.

**Dap an:** 1. read  2. had  3. did  4. didn't mention  5. had listened  6. owned  7. attend  8. weren't raining / didn't rain
