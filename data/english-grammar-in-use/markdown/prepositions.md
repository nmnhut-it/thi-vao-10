# Prepositions: Time, Place, and Collocations

## Gioi tu trong tieng Anh

Gioi tu (preposition) la mot trong nhung diem ngu phap gay nhieu loi nhat cho hoc sinh Viet Nam vi khong co quy tac tuong ung trong tieng Viet. Bai nay chia thanh 4 phan: gioi tu chi thoi gian, gioi tu chi noi chon, dong tu + gioi tu, va tinh tu + gioi tu.

---

## 1. Prepositions of Time — Gioi tu chi thoi gian

### AT / ON / IN

| Gioi tu | Dung voi | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| **AT** | Gio cu the | at 7 o'clock, at noon, at midnight |
| | Dip le ngan | at Christmas, at Easter, at Tet |
| | Cum tu co dinh | at night, at the weekend, at the moment, at present, at the same time |
| **ON** | Ngay cu the | on Monday, on 15th March, on my birthday |
| | Ngay + buoi | on Monday morning, on Friday evening |
| | Dip le co "day" | on Christmas Day, on New Year's Day, on Independence Day |
| **IN** | Thang | in January, in March, in December |
| | Nam | in 2024, in 1990 |
| | Mua | in (the) summer, in (the) winter |
| | Buoi trong ngay | in the morning, in the afternoon, in the evening |
| | Khoang thoi gian | in two weeks, in a few minutes (= sau 2 tuan / vai phut nua) |

> **Luu y quan trong:**
> - **at night** (KHONG noi ~~in night~~)
> - **in the morning** NHUNG **on Monday morning**
> - **in + khoang thoi gian** = sau bao lau nua: I'll be back **in** 10 minutes. (Toi se quay lai sau 10 phut.)

### Khong dung gioi tu truoc: this, last, next, every

- See you **next** Friday. (KHONG noi: ~~on next Friday~~)
- I went there **last** summer. (KHONG noi: ~~in last summer~~)
- We meet **every** Monday. / He came **this** morning.

### FOR / SINCE / DURING / BY / UNTIL / FROM...TO

| Gioi tu | Nghia | Dung voi | Vi du |
|---|---|---|---|
| **FOR** | Trong (bao lau) | Khoang thoi gian | I've lived here **for** 5 years. |
| **SINCE** | Tu (khi nao) | Moc thoi gian | I've lived here **since** 2019. |
| **DURING** | Trong suot | Khoang thoi gian co ten | **During** the summer, we traveled a lot. |
| **BY** | Truoc (thoi han) | Moc thoi gian | Finish it **by** Friday. (= truoc thu Sau) |
| **UNTIL / TILL** | Cho den khi | Moc thoi gian | Wait here **until** 6 o'clock. |
| **FROM...TO** | Tu...den | Hai moc thoi gian | The shop is open **from** 9 **to** 5. |

> **Phan biet FOR va SINCE:**
> - **for** + khoang thoi gian: for 3 days, for a long time, for 2 hours
> - **since** + moc thoi gian: since Monday, since 2020, since I was a child
> - Ca hai thuong di voi thi **hien tai hoan thanh** (present perfect).

> **Phan biet DURING va FOR:**
> - **during** + danh tu chi su kien/giai doan: during the lesson, during the war
> - **for** + khoang thoi gian (so luong): for two hours, for a week
> - ~~during three hours~~ (SAI) --> **for** three hours

## 2. Prepositions of Place — Gioi tu chi noi chon

### AT / ON / IN

| Gioi tu | Y nghia | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| **AT** | Tai mot diem cu the | at the bus stop, at the door, at the airport |
| | Dia chi so nha | at 25 Le Loi Street |
| | Su kien | at a party, at a concert, at a meeting |
| | Cum co dinh | at home, at work, at school, at the top, at the bottom |
| **ON** | Tren be mat | on the table, on the wall, on the floor, on the ceiling |
| | Duong/tang | on Le Loi Street, on the first floor |
| | Phuong tien cong cong | on the bus, on the train, on a plane |
| | Cum co dinh | on the left, on the right, on TV, on the phone, on the internet |
| **IN** | Ben trong | in the room, in the box, in the water |
| | Thanh pho/nuoc | in Hanoi, in Vietnam, in Asia |
| | Phuong tien nho | in a car, in a taxi |
| | Cum co dinh | in the middle, in the sky, in a row, in the newspaper, in a photo |

> **Meo quan trong:**
> - **in** a car / taxi (xe nho — ngoi "trong")
> - **on** a bus / train / plane (xe lon — dung/di lai tren do)
> - **at** home, **at** work, **at** school (KHONG co "the")

### TO / INTO

| Gioi tu | Y nghia | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| **TO** | Di den (huong toi) | go **to** school, come **to** my house, fly **to** Paris |
| **INTO** | Di vao ben trong | walk **into** the room, jump **into** the water, get **into** the car |

> **Luu y:** KHONG dung "to" truoc **home**: go home (KHONG noi: ~~go to home~~)

### Other Place Prepositions — Bang tham khao nhanh

| Gioi tu | Nghia | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| **above** | phia tren (khong cham) | The clock is above the door. |
| **below** | phia duoi (khong cham) | The valley is below the mountain. |
| **between** | giua (2 thu) | between the bank and the post office |
| **among** | giua (nhieu thu) | among the students |
| **behind** | phia sau | behind the building |
| **in front of** | phia truoc | in front of the school |
| **opposite** | doi dien | opposite the park |
| **next to / beside** | ben canh | next to the hospital |
| **near** | gan | near the river |
| **across** | bang qua | across the street |
| **along** | doc theo | along the river |
| **through** | xuyen qua | through the tunnel |
| **towards** | ve phia | walk towards the station |

## 5. Common Exam Patterns — Dang bai thuong gap

**Dang 1: Dien gioi tu thich hop**
- She arrived ___ Hanoi ___ 7 a.m. ___ Sunday.
  --> **in** Hanoi **at** 7 a.m. **on** Sunday.

**Dang 2: Chon gioi tu dung (trac nghiem)**
- I've been waiting ___ two hours. (A. since B. for C. during D. by)
  --> **B. for**

**Dang 3: Tim va sua loi sai**
- ~~She is afraid from spiders.~~ --> afraid **of**
- ~~We arrived to the station late.~~ --> arrived **at**

**Dang 4: Viet lai cau (giu nguyen nghia)**
- I started learning English in 2018. I still learn English now.
  --> I have learned English **since** 2018.

**Dang 5: Hoan thanh cau voi tu goi y**
- She / interested / learn / Japanese.
  --> She is interested **in** learning Japanese.
