# Comparison: Equal, Comparative, Superlative, and Special Patterns

## So sanh trong tieng Anh

So sanh la mot trong nhung chu diem ngu phap xuat hien nhieu nhat trong de thi vao 10 THPT. Bai nay bao gom: so sanh bang, so sanh hon, so sanh nhat, cac dang dac biet, va cac mau chuyen doi cau.

---

## 1. Equal Comparison — So sanh bang

### AS + adj/adv + AS

Dung de noi hai thu **giong nhau** ve mot tinh chat nao do.

**Cong thuc:** S + V + **as** + adj/adv + **as** + noun/pronoun/clause

- Tom is **as tall as** his father. (Tom cao bang bo.)
- She runs **as fast as** her brother.
- This test is **as difficult as** the last one.

**Dang phu dinh:** S + V + **not as/so** + adj/adv + **as**

- He is **not as tall as** his brother. (= His brother is taller than him.)
- This book isn't **as/so interesting as** that one.

> **Luu y:** Trong cau phu dinh, co the dung **so...as** hoac **as...as**. Ca hai deu dung.

### THE SAME AS

- My bag is **the same** color **as** yours.
- Her answer is **the same as** mine.

> **Bay thi:** KHONG noi ~~the same with~~ --> the same **as**

---

## 2. Comparative — So sanh hon

Dung de so sanh **hai** nguoi, vat, hoac su viec.

### Tinh tu / trang tu NGAN (1 am tiet, hoac 2 am tiet tan cung bang -y, -er, -ow, -le)

**Cong thuc:** S + V + adj/adv + **-er** + **than** + noun/pronoun

| Tinh tu goc | Dang so sanh hon | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| tall | tall**er** | He is taller than me. |
| big | bigg**er** (gap doi phu am) | This room is bigger than that one. |
| happy | happi**er** (y --> i) | She looks happier than before. |
| simple | simpl**er** | This is simpler than I thought. |
| narrow | narrow**er** | This road is narrower than Main Street. |

### Tinh tu / trang tu DAI (2 am tiet tro len)

**Cong thuc:** S + V + **more** + adj/adv + **than** + noun/pronoun

| Tinh tu goc | Dang so sanh hon | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| beautiful | **more** beautiful | She is more beautiful than her sister. |
| expensive | **more** expensive | This phone is more expensive than that one. |
| carefully | **more** carefully | Drive more carefully than last time. |
| interesting | **more** interesting | This movie is more interesting than the book. |

> **Bay thi:** KHONG noi ~~more taller~~, ~~more bigger~~, ~~more easy~~ (dung easier)

### Quy tac them -ER

| Truong hop | Quy tac | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| Phan lon | Them -er | old --> older, tall --> taller |
| Tan cung bang -e | Them -r | nice --> nicer, large --> larger |
| 1 nguyen am + 1 phu am | Gap doi phu am + er | hot --> hotter, big --> bigger, thin --> thinner |
| Tan cung bang phu am + y | Doi y thanh i + er | happy --> happier, easy --> easier |

---

## 3. Superlative — So sanh nhat

Dung de so sanh **mot** nguoi/vat voi **tat ca** nhung nguoi/vat khac trong nhom.

### Tinh tu NGAN

**Cong thuc:** S + V + **the** + adj + **-est** + (in/of)

- He is **the tallest** student **in** the class.
- This is **the hottest** day **of** the year.

### Tinh tu DAI

**Cong thuc:** S + V + **the most** + adj + (in/of)

- Ha Long Bay is **the most beautiful** place **in** Vietnam.
- She is **the most intelligent** student **of** the three.

> **Luu y:** Luon co **THE** truoc so sanh nhat.
> - ~~She is most beautiful girl.~~ (SAI)
> - She is **the** most beautiful girl. (DUNG)

### Dung IN hay OF?

| Dung **IN** | Dung **OF** |
|---|---|
| + noi chon, nhom nguoi | + khoang thoi gian, so luong |
| the tallest **in** the class | the hottest day **of** the year |
| the best **in** the world | the youngest **of** the three |
| the biggest city **in** Vietnam | the most expensive **of** all |

## 4. Irregular Forms — Dang bat quy tac

| Tinh tu / Trang tu | So sanh hon | So sanh nhat |
|---|---|---|
| good / well | **better** | **the best** |
| bad / badly | **worse** | **the worst** |
| much / many | **more** | **the most** |
| little | **less** | **the least** |
| far | **farther / further** | **the farthest / the furthest** |
| old | **older / elder** | **the oldest / the eldest** |

> **Luu y:**
> - **farther** = xa hon (khoang cach vat ly); **further** = them nua (nghia bong: further information)
> - **elder/eldest** chi dung trong gia dinh: my elder brother, the eldest son

---

## 5. Modifiers — Tu bo nghia cho so sanh

### Lam manh so sanh hon

| Tu bo nghia | Vi du |
|---|---|
| **much** | much better, much more expensive |
| **a lot** | a lot taller, a lot more interesting |
| **far** | far worse, far more difficult |
| **even** | even bigger (con... hon nua) |

### Lam yeu so sanh hon

| Tu bo nghia | Vi du |
|---|---|
| **a little** | a little cheaper |
| **a bit** | a bit older |
| **slightly** | slightly better |

- This bag is **much more expensive** than that one.
- Today is **a little warmer** than yesterday.

## 6. Double Comparatives — So sanh kep

### THE + comparative, THE + comparative (Cang...cang...)

**Cong thuc:** **The** + comparative + S + V, **the** + comparative + S + V

- **The harder** you study, **the better** results you get.
  (Cang hoc cham chi, cang dat ket qua tot.)
- **The more** you practice, **the more fluently** you speak.
- **The sooner**, **the better**. (Cang som cang tot.)
- **The older** I get, **the happier** I am.

### Comparative AND comparative (Ngay cang...)

**Cong thuc:** adj-er **and** adj-er / **more and more** + adj

- The weather is getting **colder and colder**. (Thoi tiet ngay cang lanh.)
- The city is becoming **more and more crowded**.
- Life is getting **more and more expensive**.
- She drove **faster and faster**.

---

## 7. TOO and ENOUGH

### TOO + adj/adv (Qua... khong the...)

**Cong thuc:** S + V + **too** + adj/adv + **(for sb)** + **to** + V

- This coffee is **too hot to drink**.
- The bag is **too heavy for me to carry**.
- He spoke **too quickly for us to understand**.

### ADJ/ADV + ENOUGH (Du...)

**Cong thuc:** S + V + adj/adv + **enough** + **(for sb)** + **to** + V

- She is **old enough to drive**. (Co ay du tuoi lai xe.)
- He runs **fast enough to win** the race.

### ENOUGH + noun

**Cong thuc:** S + V + **enough** + noun + **to** + V

- I don't have **enough money to buy** this phone.
- There isn't **enough time to finish** the test.

> **Bay thi:** Vi tri cua ENOUGH
> - adj/adv + **enough** (sau tinh tu): tall enough, good enough
> - **enough** + noun (truoc danh tu): enough water, enough time
> - ~~enough tall~~ (SAI) --> tall **enough**

---

## 8. SO...THAT / SUCH...THAT (Qua... den noi ma...)

### SO + adj/adv + THAT

**Cong thuc:** S + V + **so** + adj/adv + **that** + S + V

- She is **so beautiful that** everyone looks at her.
- He ran **so fast that** nobody could catch him.
- It was **so cold that** we stayed home.

### SUCH + (a/an) + adj + noun + THAT

**Cong thuc:** S + V + **such** + (a/an) + adj + noun + **that** + S + V

- She is **such a beautiful girl that** everyone looks at her.
- It was **such cold weather that** we stayed home.
- They are **such good students that** the teacher is proud.

> **Phan biet SO va SUCH:**
> - **so** + adj/adv (KHONG co danh tu theo sau)
> - **such** + (a/an) + adj + noun (CO danh tu theo sau)
> - so beautiful / such a beautiful girl
> - so cold / such cold weather

### SO MANY / SO MUCH / SO FEW / SO LITTLE

- There were **so many** people **that** we couldn't get in.
- He has **so much** money **that** he can buy anything.
- She had **so few** friends **that** she felt lonely.

## 9. Sentence Transformation Patterns — Mau chuyen doi cau

Day la phan **cuc ky quan trong** trong de thi vao 10.

### Pattern 1: So sanh hon <--> not as...as

- Tom is **taller than** Bill.
  = Bill is **not as tall as** Tom.

- This book is **more interesting than** that one.
  = That book is **not as interesting as** this one.

### Pattern 2: So sanh nhat <--> So sanh hon

- Nam is **the tallest** student in the class.
  = **No** student in the class is **taller than** Nam.
  = Nam is **taller than** any other student in the class.

### Pattern 3: TOO...TO <--> NOT + adj + ENOUGH + TO

- The water is **too hot to drink**.
  = The water is **not cool enough to drink**.

- He is **too young to drive**.
  = He is **not old enough to drive**.

### Pattern 4: TOO...TO <--> SO...THAT...CAN'T

- The box is **too heavy for me to carry**.
  = The box is **so heavy that I can't carry** it.

### Pattern 5: SUCH...THAT <--> SO...THAT

- It was **such a difficult test that** nobody passed.
  = The test was **so difficult that** nobody passed.

### Pattern 6: The same as <--> as...as

- My house is **the same height as** yours.
  = My house is **as high as** yours.

## 10. Quick Reference — Tom tat nhanh

| Cau truc | Cong thuc | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| So sanh bang | as + adj + as | as tall as |
| So sanh hon (ngan) | adj-er + than | taller than |
| So sanh hon (dai) | more + adj + than | more beautiful than |
| So sanh nhat (ngan) | the + adj-est | the tallest |
| So sanh nhat (dai) | the most + adj | the most beautiful |
| Cang...cang... | The + comp, the + comp | The more, the better |
| Ngay cang... | comp + and + comp | colder and colder |
| Qua...khong the | too + adj + to V | too hot to drink |
| Du...de | adj + enough + to V | old enough to drive |
| Qua...den noi | so + adj + that | so tired that |
| | such + (a) + adj + N + that | such a long day that |
