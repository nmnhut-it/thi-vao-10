# Conjunctions: Coordinating, Subordinating, and Correlative

## Lien tu trong tieng Anh

Lien tu (conjunction) la tu dung de noi cac tu, cum tu, hoac menh de lai voi nhau. De thi vao 10 thuong kiem tra cac lien tu chi su tuong phan, nguyen nhan - ket qua, va lien tu tuong quan (correlative conjunctions). Bai nay bao gom tat ca cac dang quan trong.

---

## 1. FANBOYS — Lien tu dang lap (Coordinating Conjunctions)

**FANBOYS** la tu viet tat cua 7 lien tu dang lap: **F**or, **A**nd, **N**or, **B**ut, **O**r, **Y**et, **S**o.

| Lien tu | Nghia | Chuc nang | Vi du |
|---|---|---|---|
| **for** | vi (= because, van viet) | Nguyen nhan | She stayed home, **for** she was ill. |
| **and** | va | Bo sung | I like tea **and** coffee. |
| **nor** | cung khong | Phu dinh bo sung | He can't swim, **nor** can she. |
| **but** | nhung | Tuong phan | It's cheap **but** good. |
| **or** | hoac | Lua chon | Tea **or** coffee? |
| **yet** | tuy nhien, nhung | Tuong phan (bat ngo) | He's rich, **yet** he's unhappy. |
| **so** | nen, vi vay | Ket qua | It rained, **so** we stayed home. |

> **Luu y:** Truoc FANBOYS khi noi hai menh de doc lap, thuong co **dau phay**.
> - I was tired, **so** I went to bed early.

## 2. Contrast — Lien tu chi su tuong phan

### ALTHOUGH / THOUGH / EVEN THOUGH + clause

Ca ba tu nay deu co nghia **"mac du"** va theo sau la **menh de** (co chu ngu + dong tu).

**Cong thuc:** **Although/Though/Even though** + S + V, S + V

- **Although** it rained heavily, we went out. (Mac du troi mua to, chung toi van ra ngoai.)
- He passed the exam **though** he didn't study hard.
- **Even though** she was tired, she kept working.

> **Muc do manh:** even though > although > though
> - **though** co the dung o cuoi cau: The food wasn't great. It was cheap, **though**. (tuy nhien)

### DESPITE / IN SPITE OF + noun / V-ing

**"Mac du"** nhung theo sau la **danh tu, cum danh tu, hoac V-ing** (KHONG phai menh de).

**Cong thuc:** **Despite / In spite of** + noun/V-ing, S + V

- **Despite** the rain, we went out.
- **In spite of** being tired, she kept working.
- He passed **despite** not studying hard.

> **Bay thi lon nhat:**
> - ~~Despite she was tired~~ (SAI — khong dung menh de)
> - **Despite being tired** hoac **Although she was tired** (DUNG)
> - ~~In spite of it rained~~ (SAI)
> - **In spite of the rain** hoac **Although it rained** (DUNG)

### DESPITE THE FACT THAT + clause

Neu muon dung menh de sau **despite/in spite of**, them **the fact that**:

- **Despite the fact that** it rained, we went out.
- **In spite of the fact that** she was tired, she kept working.

### Chuyen doi: ALTHOUGH <--> DESPITE

| Although + clause | Despite + noun/V-ing |
|---|---|
| Although he is old, ... | Despite his old age, ... / Despite being old, ... |
| Although it rained, ... | Despite the rain, ... |
| Although she was ill, ... | Despite her illness, ... / Despite being ill, ... |
| Although they tried hard, ... | Despite trying hard, ... / Despite their hard work, ... |

## 3. Cause and Result — Nguyen nhan va ket qua

### BECAUSE + clause vs. BECAUSE OF + noun/V-ing

| Cau truc | Theo sau la | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| **Because** + S + V | Menh de | **Because** it rained, we stayed home. |
| **Because of** + noun/V-ing | Danh tu / cum danh tu | **Because of** the rain, we stayed home. |

> **Bay thi:**
> - ~~Because of it rained~~ (SAI) --> **Because** it rained
> - ~~Because the rain~~ (SAI) --> **Because of** the rain

### Chuyen doi: BECAUSE <--> BECAUSE OF

- **Because** the traffic was heavy, I was late.
  = **Because of** the heavy traffic, I was late.

- **Because** he behaved badly, he was punished.
  = **Because of** his bad behavior, he was punished.

### SO vs. SO THAT

| Cau truc | Nghia | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| S + V, **so** + S + V | Nen, vi vay (ket qua) | It was cold, **so** I wore a jacket. |
| S + V + **so that** + S + V | De (ma)... (muc dich) | I study hard **so that** I can pass the exam. |

> - **so** = ket qua (consequence): It rained, so we stayed home.
> - **so that** = muc dich (purpose): I saved money **so that** I could buy a bike.
> - Sau **so that** thuong co **can/could/will/would**.

### SO...THAT / SUCH...THAT (Qua...den noi ma...)

(Xem chi tiet tai bai **Comparison**, muc 8.)

- She spoke **so quietly that** nobody heard her.
- It was **such a boring movie that** I fell asleep.

## 4. Correlative Conjunctions — Lien tu tuong quan

Cac lien tu nay di **theo cap**. Quy tac quan trong nhat la **parallel structure** (cau truc song song): hai ve phai cung loai ngu phap.

### BOTH...AND (Ca...va...)

**Cong thuc:** **Both** A **and** B + V (so nhieu)

- **Both** Tom **and** Jerry are funny.
- She is **both** intelligent **and** hardworking.
- He speaks **both** English **and** French.

> **Luu y:** Dong tu luon chia **so nhieu**: Both she and I **are** students.

### EITHER...OR (Hoac...hoac...)

**Cong thuc:** **Either** A **or** B + V (chia theo B)

- **Either** you **or** he **is** wrong. (Dong tu chia theo "he")
- **Either** my parents **or** my sister **is** coming.
- You can have **either** tea **or** coffee.

### NEITHER...NOR (Khong...cung khong...)

**Cong thuc:** **Neither** A **nor** B + V (chia theo B)

- **Neither** Tom **nor** his friends **were** there.
- **Neither** she **nor** I **am** right. (Dong tu theo "I")
- He is **neither** rich **nor** famous.

> **Quy tac chia dong tu (either...or / neither...nor):**
> Dong tu chia theo **chu ngu gan nhat** (chu ngu B).

### NOT ONLY...BUT ALSO (Khong nhung...ma con...)

**Cong thuc:** **Not only** A **but also** B + V (chia theo B)

- **Not only** students **but also** the teacher **was** surprised.
- She is **not only** beautiful **but also** intelligent.
- He **not only** sings **but also** plays the guitar.

> **Dang dao ngu (thuong gap trong de thi):**
> **Not only** + auxiliary + S + V, **but** S + **also** + V
> - **Not only does** she sing well, **but** she **also** dances beautifully.
> - **Not only did** he pass the exam, **but** he **also** got the highest score.

### NO SOONER...THAN (Vua moi...thi...)

**Cong thuc:** **No sooner** + had + S + V3/ed + **than** + S + V2/ed

- **No sooner had** I arrived **than** it started raining.
- **No sooner had** she opened the door **than** the phone rang.

> **Luu y:** Menh de voi "no sooner" dung **past perfect** (had + V3), menh de voi "than" dung **past simple**.

### HARDLY / SCARCELY...WHEN (Vua moi...thi...)

**Cong thuc:** **Hardly/Scarcely** + had + S + V3/ed + **when** + S + V2/ed

- **Hardly had** I sat down **when** the phone rang.
- **Scarcely had** we started **when** it began to rain.

> **Bay thi:** Nho dung cap:
> - no sooner ... **than** (KHONG phai ~~when~~)
> - hardly/scarcely ... **when** (KHONG phai ~~than~~)

## 5. Parallel Structure — Cau truc song song

Khi dung lien tu noi hai hoac nhieu thanh phan, cac thanh phan do **phai cung loai ngu phap**.

### Dung

- She likes **swimming** and **running**. (V-ing + V-ing)
- He is **tall**, **strong**, and **handsome**. (adj + adj + adj)
- I want **to study** hard and **to pass** the exam. (to V + to V)

### Sai

- ~~She likes **swimming** and **to run**.~~ (V-ing + to V — KHONG song song)
- ~~He is **tall**, **strong**, and **has good looks**.~~ (adj + adj + clause — KHONG song song)

### Song song voi lien tu tuong quan

- **Both** cooking **and** cleaning (noun + noun) -- DUNG
- ~~**Both** to cook **and** cleaning~~ (to V + V-ing) -- SAI
- **Not only** did she sing **but** she **also** danced (clause + clause) -- DUNG
- **Either** by bus **or** by train (prep phrase + prep phrase) -- DUNG

## 6. Sentence Transformation Patterns — Mau chuyen doi cau

### Pattern 1: ALTHOUGH <--> DESPITE

- **Although** he was sick, he went to school.
  = **Despite** being sick / his sickness, he went to school.

### Pattern 2: BECAUSE <--> BECAUSE OF

- She failed **because** she was lazy.
  = She failed **because of** her laziness / being lazy.

### Pattern 3: SO...THAT <--> TOO...TO

- The coffee was **so hot that** I couldn't drink it.
  = The coffee was **too hot** (for me) **to** drink.

### Pattern 4: SO...THAT <--> SUCH...THAT

- The test was **so difficult that** nobody passed.
  = It was **such a difficult test that** nobody passed.

### Pattern 5: ALTHOUGH <--> BUT

- **Although** he is poor, he is happy.
  = He is poor, **but** he is happy.

### Pattern 6: NOT ONLY...BUT ALSO (dao ngu)

- He is smart. He is also kind.
  = He is **not only** smart **but also** kind.
  = **Not only is** he smart, **but** he is **also** kind.

### Pattern 7: NEITHER...NOR

- He doesn't like coffee. He doesn't like tea.
  = He likes **neither** coffee **nor** tea.

### Pattern 8: NO SOONER...THAN

- I had just arrived. Then it started raining.
  = **No sooner had** I arrived **than** it started raining.

## 7. Quick Reference — Tom tat nhanh

| Cau truc | Theo sau la | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| **although / though / even though** | Menh de (S + V) | Although it rained, ... |
| **despite / in spite of** | Noun / V-ing | Despite the rain, ... |
| **because** | Menh de (S + V) | Because it rained, ... |
| **because of** | Noun / V-ing | Because of the rain, ... |
| **so** | Menh de (ket qua) | It rained, so we stayed. |
| **so that** | Menh de (muc dich) | I study so that I can pass. |
| **both...and** | A + B (V so nhieu) | Both A and B are ... |
| **either...or** | A + B (V theo B) | Either A or B is ... |
| **neither...nor** | A + B (V theo B) | Neither A nor B is ... |
| **not only...but also** | A + B (V theo B) | Not only A but also B is ... |
| **no sooner...than** | Had + V3 ... than + V2 | No sooner had I left than ... |
| **hardly...when** | Had + V3 ... when + V2 | Hardly had I sat when ... |

### Bang so sanh nhanh: Menh de vs. Cum tu

| Menh de (clause) | Cum tu (phrase) |
|---|---|
| although + S + V | despite + noun/V-ing |
| because + S + V | because of + noun/V-ing |
| though + S + V | in spite of + noun/V-ing |
