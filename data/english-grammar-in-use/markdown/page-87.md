# Unit 37 - Exercises: can/could/would you ...? etc.

## Exercise 37.1 - Which goes with which?
Match requests/offers with appropriate responses.

1. Could you pass the sugar? - e. Yes, here you are.
2. Would you like to go to the cinema? - c. Me too. Let's go out for a walk.
3-8. Match remaining pairs.

## Exercise 37.2 - Complete the sentences. Choose from box.
Options: I'd like, Can I give, Would you like to try, Would you like to come, Do you mind, Would you like, Can I take, I'd like to

1. You're driving and see a friend walking. **Can I give** you a lift?
2. You're making a cold drink. ___ ice in your drink?
3. You're ordering in a cafe. ___ a chicken salad, please.
4. You have an extra concert ticket. ___ to a concert tomorrow night?
5-8. Complete with appropriate polite expressions.

## Exercise 37.3 - What would you say in these situations?
Write appropriate requests, offers, or permission questions.

1. You can't open the door. There's a man near the door. - **Could you open the door, please?**
2. You've finished your meal and want the bill. - You ask the waiter: ___
3. You want your friend to check your English forms. - You ask: ___
4. The woman in the next room is playing loud music. - You say to her: ___
5-9. Continue writing polite expressions for various situations.
