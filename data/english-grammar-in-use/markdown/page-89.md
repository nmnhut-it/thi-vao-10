# Unit 38 - Exercises: if I do ... and if I did ...

## Exercise 38.1 - Complete with past simple from box
Words: did, dropped, found, happened, lost, was, went

1. If you **found** a wallet in the street, what would you do with it?
2. Be careful with that vase. If you ___ it, it would break into small pieces.
3-7. Complete second conditional sentences with past simple.

## Exercise 38.2 - What do you say in these situations?
Choose between first conditional (if + present, will) and second conditional (if + past, would).

1. You don't expect to win the lottery. Which do you say?
   - a. If I win the lottery, I'll buy a big house.
   - b. **If I won the lottery, I'd buy a big house.** (b is correct)
2-7. Choose the appropriate conditional for each situation.

## Exercise 38.3 - Complete the sentences
1. I'd be very scared if **somebody pointed** a gun at me.
2. I can't afford to buy a car. If ___ (I / buy) a car, I'd have to borrow the money.
3. If you had a party, who ___ (you / invite)?
4. Don't lend James your car. If ___ (he / ask) me, I wouldn't lend him mine.
5-8. Complete mixed second conditional sentences.

## Exercise 38.4 - Write sentences beginning If ...
Transform statements into second conditional sentences.

1. We're not going to take the 10.30 train. (we / arrive too early) - **If we took the 10.30 train, we'd arrive too early.**
2-5. Write second conditional sentences from given situations.
