# Communicative Exchanges - Giao Tiếp Thường Gặp Trong Đề Thi Vào 10

> Dạng bài: Chọn câu đáp lại phù hợp nhất trong hội thoại. Cần nắm các mẫu câu theo tình huống và mức độ trang trọng (formal/informal).

---

## 1. INVITATIONS (Lời mời)

### Cách mời
- **Formal:** Would you like to join us for dinner? / I'd like to invite you to my party.
- **Informal:** Do you want to come to my place? / How about coming with us?

### Chấp nhận lời mời (Accepting)
- That sounds great! I'd love to. *(thân mật)*
- That's very kind of you. I'd be delighted to. *(trang trọng)*
- Sure, why not? / Yes, I'd love to. *(trung tính)*

### Từ chối lời mời (Declining)
- I'm sorry, I can't. I have other plans. *(trung tính)*
- I'm afraid I won't be able to make it. *(trang trọng)*
- Thanks for asking, but I'm busy that day. *(thân mật)*
- That's very kind of you, but I'm afraid I can't. *(lịch sự)*

---

## 2. REQUESTS AND OFFERS (Yêu cầu và Đề nghị)

### Yêu cầu (Requests)
- **Formal:** Could you possibly help me with this? / Would you mind closing the door?
- **Informal:** Can you help me? / Give me a hand, will you?

### Đáp lại yêu cầu
- Of course. / Sure, no problem. *(đồng ý)*
- I'm sorry, but I'm afraid I can't right now. *(từ chối lịch sự)*
- **"Would you mind...?"** --> "No, not at all." (= Tôi không phiền = đồng ý) / "Of course not."

> **Bẫy thi:** "Would you mind...?" -- đáp án "No" nghĩa là ĐỒNG Ý. Nhiều học sinh chọn nhầm "Yes" vì nghĩ "Yes = đồng ý".

### Đề nghị giúp đỡ (Offers)
- Can I help you? / Would you like me to carry that?
- Shall I open the window? / Let me help you with that.

### Đáp lại lời đề nghị
- Yes, please. That's very kind of you. *(chấp nhận)*
- No, thank you. I can manage. *(từ chối lịch sự)*
- That would be great, thanks. *(chấp nhận, thân mật)*

---

## 3. SUGGESTIONS (Gợi ý)

### Cách gợi ý
- Why don't we go to the cinema? / How about going swimming?
- Shall we eat out tonight? / Let's visit the museum.
- I suggest (that) we take a taxi. / What about trying the new restaurant?

### Đồng ý với gợi ý
- That's a good idea! / Great idea! *(thân mật)*
- Yes, let's do that. / Sounds good to me. *(trung tính)*
- That sounds wonderful. *(trang trọng hơn)*

### Không đồng ý với gợi ý
- I don't think that's a good idea. / I'd rather stay home.
- Well, I'd prefer to do something else. / How about ... instead?

---

## 4. APOLOGIES (Xin lỗi)

### Cách xin lỗi
- I'm sorry (for/about ...). / I apologize for the delay.
- I'm really sorry. I didn't mean to hurt you. / Please forgive me.

### Đáp lại lời xin lỗi
- That's OK. / Never mind. / Don't worry about it. *(thân mật)*
- It doesn't matter. / No harm done. *(trung tính)*
- Apology accepted. *(trang trọng)*

---

## 5. THANKS (Cảm ơn)

### Cách cảm ơn
- Thank you (so much). / Thanks a lot. / I really appreciate it.
- That's very kind/nice of you. / I'm grateful for your help.

### Đáp lại lời cảm ơn
- You're welcome. / Not at all. *(trung tính)*
- Don't mention it. / My pleasure. *(trang trọng hơn)*
- No problem. / Anytime. *(thân mật)*

---

## 6. OPINIONS - AGREEING AND DISAGREEING (Ý kiến)

### Hỏi ý kiến
- What do you think about/of ...? / How do you feel about ...?
- Do you agree that ...? / What's your opinion on ...?

### Đồng ý (Agreeing)
- I agree (with you). / You're absolutely right. *(trung tính)*
- I couldn't agree more. / That's exactly what I think. *(mạnh)*
- I think so too. / Definitely. *(thân mật)*

### Không đồng ý (Disagreeing)
- I'm afraid I disagree. / I don't think so. *(lịch sự)*
- I see your point, but ... / I'm not sure about that. *(nhẹ nhàng)*
- I totally disagree. *(mạnh -- ít dùng trong thi)*

---

## 7. GREETINGS AND FAREWELLS (Chào hỏi)

### Chào hỏi
- How are you? / How are you doing? / How's it going?
- **Formal:** How do you do? *(chỉ dùng lần gặp đầu tiên, đáp lại: "How do you do?")*

### Đáp lại
- I'm fine, thanks. And you? / Not bad, thanks. / Pretty good.
- **Lưu ý:** "How do you do?" --> "How do you do?" (KHÔNG trả lời "I'm fine")

### Tạm biệt
- Goodbye. / See you later. / Take care. / Have a nice day.

---

## 8. COMPLIMENTS (Khen ngợi)

### Cách khen
- What a lovely dress! / You look great today!
- Your English is very good. / That was an excellent presentation.

### Đáp lại lời khen
- Thank you. That's very nice of you to say. *(lịch sự)*
- Thanks! / Oh, do you really think so? *(thân mật)*
- Thank you. I'm glad you like it. *(trung tính)*

> **Lưu ý:** Người Việt thường khiêm tốn từ chối lời khen, nhưng trong tiếng Anh chuẩn, nên nói "Thank you" trước.

---

## 9. ASKING FOR AND GIVING DIRECTIONS (Hỏi/Chỉ đường)

### Hỏi đường
- Excuse me, could you tell me the way to the post office?
- How can I get to the train station from here?
- Is there a bank near here? / Do you know where the hospital is?

### Chỉ đường
- Go straight ahead, then turn left at the traffic lights.
- It's on your right, opposite the park. / Take the second turning on the right.
- It's about 500 meters from here. You can't miss it.

---

## 10. MAKING COMPLAINTS (Phàn nàn)

### Cách phàn nàn
- I'm afraid there's a problem with ... / I'd like to make a complaint about ...
- Excuse me, but this food is cold. / I'm not satisfied with the service.

### Đáp lại lời phàn nàn
- I'm terribly sorry about that. I'll deal with it right away. *(trang trọng)*
- Sorry about that. Let me fix it for you. *(trung tính)*
- I apologize for the inconvenience. *(trang trọng)*

---

## 11. GIVING ADVICE (Cho lời khuyên)

### Cách cho lời khuyên
- You should / ought to study harder. / If I were you, I would talk to her.
- Why don't you see a doctor? / You'd better not stay up late.
- I think you should ... / Have you thought about ...?

### Đáp lại
- That's good advice. I'll try that. / You're right. I should.
- I'll think about it. / Thanks for the advice, but I've already tried that.

---

## 12. EXPRESSING SYMPATHY (Thể hiện sự thông cảm)

### Khi nghe tin buồn
- I'm sorry to hear that. / That's too bad. / What a shame!
- I hope you feel better soon. *(khi ai đó ốm)*
- Please accept my condolences. *(chia buồn, trang trọng)*

### Đáp lại
- Thank you for your concern. / That's very kind of you.
- Thanks. I appreciate it.

---

## Mẹo Làm Bài Thi (Exam Tips)

1. **Đọc cả lượt A lẫn lượt B** -- đáp án phải khớp về chủ đề VÀ mức độ trang trọng.
2. **Chú ý từ khóa:** "Would you mind...?" cần "No/Not at all"; "How do you do?" cần "How do you do?"
3. **Loại trừ đáp án vô lý:** Nếu A hỏi về thời tiết, B không thể trả lời về sức khỏe.
4. **Đáp án lịch sự thường đúng hơn** đáp án cộc lốc trong ngữ cảnh thi cử.
5. **Cảnh giác với các cặp dễ nhầm:**
   - "Would you like ...?" (mời) vs. "Do you like ...?" (hỏi sở thích)
   - "Shall I ...?" (đề nghị giúp) vs. "Shall we ...?" (gợi ý cùng làm)
