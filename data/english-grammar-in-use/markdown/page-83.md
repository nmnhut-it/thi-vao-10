# Unit 35 - Exercises: I'd better ... it's time ...

## Exercise 35.1 - Write 'd better or 'd better not + verb
Verbs: check, disturb, go, put, reserve, take

1. It looks as if it might rain. **We'd better take** an umbrella.
2. You and Kate plan to go to a restaurant tonight. We ___ a table.
3. Oliver has just cut himself. You ___ on it.
4. Rebecca doesn't look well enough to go to work. You ___ this morning.
5. You're not sure what time the film starts. You ___ the film starts.
6. You need to talk to your boss, but she's very busy right now. You ___ right now.

## Exercise 35.2 - Is had better OK? Change to should where necessary.
1. I have an appointment in ten minutes. I'd better go now or I'll be late. - **OK**
2. You'd better set your alarm. You have to get up early tomorrow.
3. I'm glad you came to see us. You'd better come more often.
4-7. Check and correct where had better should be should.

## Exercise 35.3 - Complete the sentences. Choose from box.
Mixed exercise with better, do, did, had, hadn't, I'd, not, to, take, should, was, were etc.

1. It might rain. We'd better **take** an umbrella.
2-12. Complete mixed sentences using had better, should, and it's time.

## Exercise 35.4 - Write sentences with It's time (somebody did something)
1. You planned to go home at 11 o'clock. It's already 11 o'clock now. - **It's time I went home.**
2-6. Write "it's time" sentences from situational descriptions.
