# Articles: A/An, The, and Zero Article

## Mao tu trong tieng Anh (Quan tu)

Tieng Viet khong co mao tu, nen day la mot trong nhung diem ngu phap kho nhat cho hoc sinh Viet Nam. Bai nay giup em nam vung cach dung **a/an**, **the**, va khi nao **khong dung mao tu**.

---

## 1. A / AN — Mao tu khong xac dinh (Indefinite Article)

Dung **a/an** khi noi ve mot nguoi hoac mot vat **bat ky**, nguoi nghe **chua biet cu the** la ai/cai nao.

### Quy tac chon A hay AN: dua vao AM THANH, khong phai chu cai

| Dung **a** truoc am PHU AM | Dung **an** truoc am NGUYEN AM |
|---|---|
| a **b**ook, a **c**at, a **d**og | an **a**pple, an **e**gg, an **i**dea |
| a **u**niversity (/juː/ — am phu am) | an **u**mbrella (/ʌ/ — am nguyen am) |
| a **u**niform (/juː/) | an **u**nusual day (/ʌ/) |
| a **E**uropean country (/jʊə/) | an **h**our (/aʊə/ — h cam) |
| a **o**ne-way street (/wʌn/) | an **h**onest person (/ɒn/) |
| a **h**ouse (/h/ phat am) | an **MP3** player (/em/) |

> **Meo thi:** Dung nham "a university" thanh "an university" la loi rat pho bien. Hay nho: NGHE am dau, khong nhin chu cai dau.

### Khi nao dung A/AN

**a) Lan dau nhac den mot thu gi do:**
- I saw **a** dog in the park. (Nguoi nghe chua biet con cho nao.)
- She is **a** teacher. (Gioi thieu nghe nghiep.)

**b) Voi nghe nghiep, quoc tich, ton giao:**
- He is **an** engineer. / She is **a** Buddhist.
- My father is **a** doctor. (KHONG noi: ~~My father is doctor.~~)

**c) Voi so luong — nghia "mot":**
- I'd like **a** cup of coffee. (= one cup)
- Wait **a** minute. / half **an** hour

**d) Voi "what" trong cau cam than + danh tu dem duoc so it:**
- What **a** beautiful day!
- What **an** interesting story!

**e) Voi tan suat va gia ca (nghia "moi"):**
- three times **a** week (ba lan mot tuan)
- 60 kilometres **an** hour
- $5 **a** kilo

## 2. THE — Mao tu xac dinh (Definite Article)

Dung **the** khi ca nguoi noi va nguoi nghe deu **biet ro** dang noi ve ai/cai gi.

### Cac truong hop dung THE

**a) Da nhac den truoc do:**
- I saw **a** dog in the park. **The** dog was very friendly.
- (Lan 1: a dog — chua biet. Lan 2: the dog — da biet con cho nao.)

**b) Chi co mot — duy nhat (unique):**
- **the** sun, **the** moon, **the** earth, **the** sky, **the** world, **the** internet
- **the** government, **the** police, **the** environment

**c) Voi so sanh nhat (superlative):**
- **the** tallest building in the city
- **the** most beautiful girl in the class
- **the** best student in our school

**d) Voi so thu tu (ordinal numbers):**
- **the** first time, **the** second floor, **the** last page

**e) Voi nhac cu (musical instruments):**
- play **the** guitar, play **the** piano, play **the** violin
- (So sanh: play football — KHONG co the — vi la mon the thao)

**f) Voi ten song, bien, dai duong, day nui, quan dao, sa mac:**
- **the** Red River (song Hong), **the** Pacific Ocean, **the** Alps, **the** Sahara

**g) Voi ten nuoc co "Republic/Kingdom/States":**
- **the** United States, **the** United Kingdom, **the** Czech Republic

**h) Voi ten nuoc so nhieu:**
- **the** Philippines, **the** Netherlands

**i) Voi ho gia dinh (so nhieu):**
- **the** Smiths (= gia dinh nha Smith), **the** Nguyens

**j) Voi "the + tinh tu" chi mot nhom nguoi:**
- **the** rich (nguoi giau), **the** poor, **the** elderly, **the** young, **the** unemployed

## 3. Zero Article — Khong dung mao tu

### Khong dung a/an/the truoc:

**a) Bua an (meals):**
- have **breakfast** / **lunch** / **dinner**
- (NHUNG: The breakfast at this hotel is excellent. — noi cu the bua sang nao)

**b) Mon the thao va tro choi:**
- play **football**, **basketball**, **chess**, **badminton**

**c) Mon hoc va nganh hoc:**
- study **Math**, **English**, **History**, **Physics**

**d) Ngon ngu:**
- speak **Vietnamese**, learn **English**, understand **French**

**e) Ten nuoc, thanh pho, chau luc (phan lon):**
- **Vietnam**, **Hanoi**, **Asia**, **Europe**, **France**

**f) Phuong tien giao thong voi "by":**
- by **bus**, by **train**, by **car**, by **plane**, by **bike**
- (NHUNG: in **the** car, on **the** bus — khi noi vi tri cu the)

**g) Ten nui don le, ho don le, dao don le:**
- **Mount Everest**, **Lake Baikal**, **Cat Ba Island**

**h) Ten duong, quang truong, cong vien, nha ga, san bay:**
- **Oxford Street**, **Times Square**, **Hyde Park**, **Tan Son Nhat Airport**

## 4. Special Patterns: School, Hospital, Prison, Church, Bed, Work, Home

Day la nhom tu co nghia thay doi tuy theo co THE hay khong:

| Khong co THE (muc dich chinh) | Co THE (dia diem vat ly) |
|---|---|
| go to **school** (di hoc) | go to **the school** (den toa nha truong) |
| go to **hospital** (di kham/nham vien) | go to **the hospital** (den benh vien — tham ai do) |
| go to **prison** (di tu) | go to **the prison** (den nha tu — tham tu nhan) |
| go to **church** (di le nha tho) | go to **the church** (den toa nha nha tho) |
| go to **bed** (di ngu) | sit on **the bed** (ngoi tren giuong) |
| go to **work** (di lam) | — |
| go **home** (ve nha) | — |

> **Vi du trong de thi:**
> - My brother is in **hospital**. (Anh toi dang nam vien.)
> - I went to **the hospital** to visit my brother. (Toi den benh vien tham anh.)

## 5. Exam Traps — Bay trong de thi vao 10

### Bay 1: A hay AN truoc chu "H"
- **an** hour, **an** honest man (h cam — dung AN)
- **a** house, **a** hotel, **a** happy child (h phat am — dung A)

### Bay 2: A hay AN truoc chu "U"
- **a** university, **a** uniform, **a** useful tool (/juː/ — am phu am)
- **an** umbrella, **an** ugly house, **an** unusual event (/ʌ/ — am nguyen am)

### Bay 3: THE voi so sanh nhat
- Ha Long Bay is **the** most beautiful bay in Vietnam. (PHAI co THE)
- ~~Ha Long Bay is most beautiful bay in Vietnam.~~ (SAI)

### Bay 4: Khong dung THE truoc mon hoc / ngon ngu
- I like **English**. (KHONG noi: ~~I like the English.~~)
- **Math** is my favorite subject.

### Bay 5: THE truoc nhac cu, KHONG co THE truoc the thao
- She plays **the** piano very well.
- He plays **football** every Sunday.

### Bay 6: Nghe nghiep PHAI co A/AN
- She is **a** nurse. (KHONG noi: ~~She is nurse.~~)
- He wants to be **an** architect.

## 7. Practice Patterns — Dang bai tap thuong gap

**Dang 1: Chon a, an, the, hoac khong dien (zero article)**
- She plays ___ piano beautifully. --> **the**
- We had ___ lunch at noon. --> **(khong dien gi)**
- He is ___ honest man. --> **an**

**Dang 2: Tim loi sai**
- ~~I go to school by the bus.~~ --> I go to school by bus.
- ~~She is doctor.~~ --> She is **a** doctor.
- ~~The Mount Everest is the highest mountain.~~ --> **Mount Everest** is the highest mountain.

**Dang 3: Viet lai cau**
- No river in Vietnam is longer than the Mekong River.
  --> The Mekong River is **the longest** river in Vietnam.
