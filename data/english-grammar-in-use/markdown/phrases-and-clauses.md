# Phrases and Clauses (Cum tu va Menh de)

Tai lieu ngu phap danh cho hoc sinh lop 9 on thi vao 10 THPT.

## 1. Adverb Clauses (Menh de trang ngu)

Menh de trang ngu bo sung thong tin ve thoi gian, ly do, muc dich, ket qua, hoac su nhuong bo cho menh de chinh. Menh de trang ngu co the dung dau hoac cuoi cau.

### 1.1. Time Clauses (Menh de chi thoi gian)

| Lien tu | Nghia | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| **when** | khi | **When** I arrived, everyone had left. |
| **while** | trong khi | **While** she was cooking, the phone rang. |
| **before** | truoc khi | Finish your homework **before** you go out. |
| **after** | sau khi | **After** he had dinner, he watched TV. |
| **as soon as** | ngay khi | I'll call you **as soon as** I get home. |
| **until / till** | cho den khi | Wait here **until** I come back. |
| **as** | khi, trong khi | **As** I was walking home, I met an old friend. |
| **since** | tu khi | I haven't seen her **since** she moved away. |
| **by the time** | truoc luc, den luc | **By the time** we arrived, the film had started. |

> **Quy tac quan trong:** Trong menh de chi thoi gian noi ve **tuong lai**, dung **thi hien tai** (Present Simple hoac Present Perfect), **KHONG dung "will"**:
> - SAI: When I **will arrive**, I'll call you.
> - DUNG: When I **arrive**, I'll call you.
> - DUNG: I'll wait until she **comes** back.
> - DUNG: As soon as I **have finished**, I'll let you know.

### 1.2. Reason Clauses (Menh de chi nguyen nhan)

| Lien tu | Nghia | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| **because** | boi vi | She stayed home **because** she was sick. |
| **since** | vi (da biet) | **Since** you're here, let's start. |
| **as** | vi (da biet) | **As** it was raining, we cancelled the picnic. |

**Phan biet:**
- **Because** la pho bien nhat, dung trong moi tinh huong.
- **Since** va **as** thuong dat dau cau, dung khi ly do la dieu nguoi nghe da biet.

> **Lien quan:** "Because of" + danh tu / V-ing (khong phai menh de):
> - She stayed home **because of** the rain. (khong phai "because of it rained")
> - He failed **because of** not studying hard.

### 1.3. Purpose Clauses (Menh de chi muc dich)

| Cau truc | Nghia | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| **so that** + S + can/could/will/would | de ma | She studies hard **so that** she **can** pass the exam. |
| **in order that** + S + can/could | de ma | He left early **in order that** he **could** catch the train. |
| **to / in order to / so as to** + V | de | She studies hard **(in order) to** pass the exam. |
| **so as not to / in order not to** + V | de khong | He spoke softly **so as not to** wake the baby. |

> **Luu y:** "So that" theo sau boi menh de (S + V). "To / In order to" theo sau boi dong tu nguyen mau.

### 1.4. Result Clauses (Menh de chi ket qua)

#### so ... that (qua ... den noi ma)

**S + V + so + adj/adv + that + S + V**

- The exam was **so** difficult **that** many students failed.
- She spoke **so** fast **that** I couldn't understand her.
- He worked **so** hard **that** he got the best grade.

#### such ... that (qua ... den noi ma)

**S + V + such + (a/an) + adj + noun + that + S + V**

- It was **such** a difficult exam **that** many students failed.
- They are **such** nice people **that** everyone likes them.
- She made **such** rapid progress **that** the teacher was amazed.

**Cach phan biet so va such:**
- **So** + adj/adv (khong co danh tu theo sau): so beautiful, so quickly
- **Such** + (a/an) + adj + noun: such a beautiful day, such nice weather

> **Meo thi:** Neu sau cho trong co "a/an + adj + noun" -> dung **such**. Neu chi co adj/adv -> dung **so**.

### 1.5. Concession Clauses (Menh de chi su nhuong bo)

Dien dat y "mac du ... nhung ...".

| Cau truc | Theo sau boi | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| **although / though / even though** | menh de (S + V) | **Although** it rained, we had a good time. |
| **despite / in spite of** | danh tu / V-ing | **Despite** the rain, we had a good time. |

**Vi du chi tiet:**

- **Although** she was tired, she kept working.
- **Though** the hotel wasn't great, I liked the location. (though = although)
- **Even though** I was really tired, I couldn't sleep. (even though manh hon although)
- **Despite being** tired, she kept working. (despite + V-ing)
- **In spite of the** bad weather, we went out. (in spite of + noun)

#### Chuyen doi giua although va despite/in spite of

Day la dang bai **cuc ky pho bien** trong de thi vao 10:

| Although + clause | Despite / In spite of + noun/V-ing |
|---|---|
| Although it **rained** heavily ... | Despite / In spite of **the heavy rain** ... |
| Although she **was** ill ... | Despite / In spite of **her illness** ... |
| Although he **tried** hard ... | Despite / In spite of **trying** hard ... |
| Although the traffic **was** bad ... | Despite / In spite of **the bad traffic** ... |

**Cach chuyen doi:**
1. Bo chu ngu va dong tu "be" -> giu lai cum danh tu:
   - Although the weather **was** bad -> Despite **the bad weather**
2. Chuyen dong tu thanh V-ing:
   - Although he **studied** hard -> Despite **studying** hard
3. Chuyen "adj" thanh "noun":
   - Although she was **ill** -> Despite her **illness**

> **Luu y:** "Despite" KHONG co "of". "In spite" LUON co "of".
> - SAI: Despite **of** the rain...
> - SAI: In spite the rain...
> - DUNG: **Despite** the rain... / **In spite of** the rain...

#### Though o cuoi cau

Trong giao tiep va bai thi, "though" co the dung o cuoi cau (= nhung ma):
- The house isn't very nice. I like the garden, **though**. (= but I like the garden)
- I see them every day. I've never spoken to them, **though**.

## 2. Noun Clauses (Menh de danh tu)

Menh de danh tu dong vai tro nhu mot danh tu trong cau: lam chu ngu, tan ngu, hoac bo ngu.

### 2.1. That-clauses (Menh de bat dau bang "that")

- Lam tan ngu: I **think (that)** she is right. ("that" co the luoc bo)
- Lam chu ngu: **That he passed** the exam surprised everyone.
- Sau tinh tu: I'm glad **(that)** you came.
- Sau "It is + adj": **It** is important **(that)** students attend class regularly.

**Vi du:**
- I believe **(that)** honesty is the best policy.
- Everyone knows **(that)** the earth goes round the sun.
- **It** is surprising **(that)** he didn't come.

### 2.2. Wh-clauses (Menh de bat dau bang tu hoi)

Dung when, where, what, who, which, why, how, whether, if lam menh de danh tu.

> **Quy tac quan trong:** Trong menh de danh tu, dung **trat tu cau tran thuat** (S + V), KHONG dung trat tu cau hoi:
> - SAI: I don't know **where is she**.
> - DUNG: I don't know **where she is**.
> - SAI: Can you tell me **what time does the train leave**?
> - DUNG: Can you tell me **what time the train leaves**?

**Vi du:**
- I wonder **why he didn't come**.
- Do you know **where she lives**?
- Tell me **what you think** about this.
- **How he solved the problem** is a mystery.
- I'm not sure **whether/if** she will come.

## 3. Participle Phrases (Cum phan tu)

Cum phan tu la dang rut gon cua menh de quan he (relative clause) hoac menh de trang ngu. Co hai dang chinh:

### 3.1. Present participle phrases (-ing clauses)

Rut gon menh de quan he o the **chu dong**:

| Menh de day du | Rut gon bang -ing |
|---|---|
| The woman **who is talking** to Tom is my teacher. | The woman **talking** to Tom is my teacher. |
| The boy **who sits** next to me is very smart. | The boy **sitting** next to me is very smart. |
| Do you know the girl **who is standing** over there? | Do you know the girl **standing** over there? |

**Cach rut gon:** Bo "who/which/that" + bo tro dong tu -> giu V-ing.

Cum -ing cung dung thay cho menh de trang ngu chi thoi gian hoac ly do:
- **Walking** home, I saw an accident. (= While I was walking home...)
- **Being** tired, she went to bed early. (= Because she was tired...)
- **Not knowing** what to do, he called his friend. (= Because he didn't know...)

### 3.2. Past participle phrases (-ed / V3 clauses)

Rut gon menh de quan he o the **bi dong**:

| Menh de day du | Rut gon bang -ed/V3 |
|---|---|
| The boy **who was injured** in the accident is in hospital. | The boy **injured** in the accident is in hospital. |
| The books **which were written** by Nguyen Nhat Anh are popular. | The books **written** by Nguyen Nhat Anh are popular. |
| The letter **that was sent** yesterday hasn't arrived. | The letter **sent** yesterday hasn't arrived. |

**Cach rut gon:** Bo "who/which/that" + bo "was/were/is/are" -> giu V3/ed.

**Vi du them:**
- Most of the goods **made** in this factory are exported. (= goods that are made...)
- The police arrested a man **seen** near the crime scene. (= a man who was seen...)

### 3.3. Phan biet -ing va -ed

| -ing (chu dong) | -ed/V3 (bi dong) |
|---|---|
| The man **driving** the car... (nguoi lai xe) | The car **driven** by the man... (xe duoc lai) |
| The students **taking** the exam... (hoc sinh dang lam bai) | The exam **taken** by the students... (bai thi duoc lam) |

**Meo:** Hoi "ai/cai gi lam hanh dong?" -> dung -ing. Hoi "ai/cai gi bi/duoc lam?" -> dung -ed/V3.

## 4. Bang tom tat cac cau truc

| Loai | Lien tu / Cau truc | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| Thoi gian | when, while, before, after, as soon as, until | When I arrive, I'll call you. |
| Nguyen nhan | because, since, as | Because it rained, we stayed home. |
| Muc dich | so that, in order to, so as to | She studies hard so that she can pass. |
| Ket qua | so...that, such...that | It was so cold that we stayed inside. |
| Nhuong bo | although, despite, in spite of | Although it rained, we went out. |
| Menh de danh tu | that, what, where, how, whether | I know that she is right. |
| Cum phan tu | V-ing (chu dong), V3/ed (bi dong) | The man sitting here is my dad. |

## 5. Cac dang de thi thuong gap

### Dang 1: Noi hai cau thanh mot (dung lien tu)

**De:** It rained heavily. We still went to school.
- **Although** it rained heavily, we still went to school.
- **In spite of** the heavy rain, we still went to school.
- **Despite** the heavy rain, we still went to school.

### Dang 2: Viet lai cau (chuyen doi cau truc)

**De:** Although she was sick, she went to school. -> Rewrite using "Despite".
**Dap an:** **Despite being** sick, she went to school. / **Despite her sickness**, she went to school.

**De:** The movie was so boring that I fell asleep. -> Rewrite using "such".
**Dap an:** It was **such** a boring movie **that** I fell asleep.

**De:** She studied hard. She wanted to pass the exam. -> Combine using "so that".
**Dap an:** She studied hard **so that** she could pass the exam.

### Dang 3: Rut gon menh de quan he

**De:** The man who is standing at the door is my uncle.
**Dap an:** The man **standing** at the door is my uncle.

**De:** The bridge which was built in 1990 needs repairing.
**Dap an:** The bridge **built** in 1990 needs repairing.

### Dang 4: Chon tu/cum tu dung

**De:** ______ the bad weather, the football match was not cancelled.
- A. Although  B. In spite of  C. Because  D. Because of
**Dap an:** B. In spite of (theo sau la cum danh tu "the bad weather")

**De:** The test was ______ difficult that nobody could finish it.
- A. such  B. very  C. too  D. so
**Dap an:** D. so (so + adj + that)

## 6. Loi sai thuong gap

| Sai | Dung | Giai thich |
|---|---|---|
| **Despite of** the rain... | **Despite** the rain... | Despite khong co "of" |
| **In spite** the rain... | **In spite of** the rain... | In spite luon di voi "of" |
| **Although** the rain, we went out. | **Despite** the rain... HOAC **Although** it rained... | Although + clause; Despite + noun |
| When I **will arrive**... | When I **arrive**... | Menh de thoi gian khong dung "will" |
| I don't know **where is she**. | I don't know **where she is**. | Menh de danh tu: trat tu tran thuat |
| It was **so** a boring movie that... | It was **such** a boring movie that... | Such + a/an + adj + noun |
| The woman **who standing** there... | The woman **standing** there... | Rut gon: bo who, dung V-ing |

## 7. Bai tap tu luyen

**Dien tu hoac viet lai cau:**

1. ______ (Although / Despite) being very tired, he finished the project.
2. The weather was ______ bad ______ we couldn't go out. (so...that)
3. She is studying hard ______ (so that / so as to) she can get a scholarship.
4. ______ (When / While) I will see him, I'll give him your message. (Sua loi)
5. The girl (who is) ______ (sit) next to me is my best friend. (rut gon)
6. The house (which was) ______ (build) in 1920 is still beautiful. (rut gon)
7. I couldn't sleep ______ (because / because of) the noise.
8. ______ it was very cold, she didn't wear a coat. (Although / Despite)

**Dap an:**
1. Despite  2. so ... that  3. so that (theo sau la menh de) / so as to (theo sau la dong tu)  4. Sua: **When** I **see** him (khong dung "will" trong menh de thoi gian)  5. sitting  6. built  7. because of  8. Although
