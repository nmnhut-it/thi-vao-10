# Unit 36 - Exercises: would

## Exercise 36.1 - Write about yourself (would like / wouldn't like)
1. A place you'd love to live: **I'd love to live by the sea.**
2-5. Write about jobs, activities, things you would/wouldn't like.

## Exercise 36.2 - Complete with would + verb (correct form)
Verbs: be, do, enjoy, have, stop

1. They helped us a lot. I don't know what we **would have done** without their help.
2-8. Complete sentences using would (do) or would have (done).

## Exercise 36.3 - Match sentences on left with follow-ups on right
Match present/past situations with appropriate would/would have responses.

1. I'd like to go to Australia one day. - c. It would be nice.
2. I wouldn't like to live on a busy road. - (match)
3-6. Continue matching.

## Exercise 36.4 - Write sentences using promised + would/wouldn't
1. I wonder why Laura is late. **She promised she wouldn't be late.**
2-4. Write promised + would/wouldn't sentences.

## Exercise 36.5 - Complete with wouldn't + verb
Express refusal using wouldn't.

1. I tried to warn him, but he **wouldn't listen** to me.
2-4. Complete refusal sentences.

## Exercise 36.6 - Complete using would (past habits)
Verbs: forget, shake, share, smile, stay, walk

1. Whenever Richard was angry, he **would walk** out of the room.
2-6. Complete sentences about regular past actions using would.
