# Unit 41 - Exercises: wish

## Exercise 41.1 - Put in wish(ed) or hope(d)
1. I **wish** you a pleasant stay at this hotel.
2. Enjoy your holiday. I ___ you have a great time.
3. Goodbye. I ___ you all the best for the future.
4-7. Choose between wish and hope.

## Exercise 41.2 - Complete the sentences
1. Jack is going on a trip to Mexico soon. I wish I **was going** too.
2. I'm very tired and I have so much to do. I wish I ___ so tired.
3. You didn't tell me you were ill. Why not? I wish you ___ me.
4-8. Complete wish sentences expressing present and past regret.

## Exercise 41.3 - Write sentences with I wish ... would
Express complaints or desires for change.

1. It's raining. You want to go out. - **I wish it would stop raining.**
2. You're waiting for Jane. She's late. - I wish she ___
3. You're looking for a job. Nobody will give you one. - I wish somebody ___
4. A dog has been barking. You're trying to study. - ___

**With I wish ... wouldn't:**
5. Your friend drives very fast. - I wish you ___
6. Joe leaves the door open all the time. - ___
7. A lot of people drop litter. - I wish people ___

## Exercise 41.4 - Put the verb into the correct form
Mixed wish exercise with all forms.

1. It was a stupid thing to say. I wish **I hadn't said** it. (I / not / say)
2. I'm fed up with this rain. I wish **it would stop**. (it / stop)
3-14. Complete sentences using wish + past, wish + past perfect, wish + would, wish + could.
