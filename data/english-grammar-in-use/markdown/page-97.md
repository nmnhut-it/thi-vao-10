# Unit 42 - Exercises: Passive 1

## Exercise 42.1 - Complete with passive verbs (present or past)
Verbs: cause, damage, find, hold, injure, invite, make, overtake, own, send, show, surround

1. Many accidents **are caused** by careless driving.
2. Cheese ___ from milk.
3. The roof of the building ___ in a storm a few days ago.
4-12. Complete sentences with correct passive form (present or past).

## Exercise 42.2 - Write questions using the passive
Some present, some past.

1. Ask about glass. (how / make?) - **How is glass made?**
2. Ask about television. (when / invent?) - When ___?
3. Ask about mountains. (how / form?) - ___?
4-5. Continue writing passive questions.

## Exercise 42.3 - Put the verb into the correct form (active or passive)
1a. Two hundred people **are employed** (employ) by the company.
1b. The company **employs** (employ) 200 people.
2-9. Choose active or passive form based on context. Each pair contrasts active and passive.

## Exercise 42.4 - Rewrite using passive
Transform active sentences (with somebody/they/people) into passive.

1. Somebody cleans the room every day. - **The room is cleaned every day.**
2. They cancelled all flights because of fog. - All ___
3. Somebody accused me of stealing money. - ___
4-8. Continue transforming to passive.
