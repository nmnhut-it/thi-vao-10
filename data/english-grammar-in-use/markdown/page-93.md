# Unit 40 - Exercises: if I had known ... I wish I had known ...

## Exercise 40.1 - Put the verb into the correct form
1. I didn't see you. If **I'd seen** (I / see) you, **I would have said** (I / say) hello.
2. Sarah got to the station just in time. If ___ (she / miss) the train, ___ (she / miss) her flight too.
3. Thanks for reminding me about Lisa's birthday. ___ (I / forget) if ___ (you / not / remind) me.
4-9. Complete third conditional sentences with correct forms.

## Exercise 40.2 - Write a sentence beginning with If
Transform past situations into third conditional sentences.

1. I wasn't hungry, so I didn't eat anything. - **If I'd been hungry, I would have eaten something.**
2. The accident happened because the road was icy.
3. I didn't know that you had to get up early, so I didn't wake you up.
4-8. Continue writing third conditional sentences.

## Exercise 40.3 - Write sentences with I wish
Express regret about past events.

1. You've eaten too much and now you feel sick. - **I wish I hadn't eaten so much.**
2. When you were younger, you never learned to play a musical instrument.
3. You've painted the gate red. Now you think red was the wrong colour.
4-6. Continue writing I wish + past perfect sentences.
