# Quantifiers (Tu chi luong)

Tai lieu ngu phap danh cho hoc sinh lop 9 on thi vao 10 THPT.

---

## 1. Danh tu dem duoc va khong dem duoc (Countable vs Uncountable)

Truoc khi hoc quantifiers, can phan biet ro hai loai danh tu:

| Countable (dem duoc) | Uncountable (khong dem duoc) |
|---|---|
| book, student, car, idea | water, money, information, advice |
| Co dang so nhieu: books, students | Khong co dang so nhieu |
| Dung a/an: a book, an idea | Khong dung a/an |

> **Luu y:** Mot so danh tu VN tuong la dem duoc nhung tieng Anh lai khong: **information, advice, news, furniture, luggage, homework, equipment, knowledge, progress, research, weather, traffic, work** (cong viec).

## 2. Some va Any

### 2.1. Quy tac co ban

| | Countable (so nhieu) | Uncountable |
|---|---|---|
| **Some** (mot vai, mot it) | some books | some water |
| **Any** (bat ky, nao) | any books | any water |

- **Some**: dung trong cau **khang dinh** va cau hoi khi **moi** hoac **yeu cau**:
  - I have **some** friends in Hanoi.
  - Would you like **some** coffee? (loi moi)
  - Can I have **some** water? (yeu cau)

- **Any**: dung trong cau **phu dinh** va cau **hoi**:
  - I don't have **any** money.
  - Do you have **any** questions?
  - She left without **any** explanation.

- **Any** mang nghia "bat ky" trong cau khang dinh:
  - You can sit in **any** seat you like. (bat ky ghe nao)
  - **Any** student can join the club.

### 2.2. Cac tu ghep voi some/any/no

| | -body / -one (nguoi) | -thing (vat) | -where (noi) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **some-** | somebody / someone | something | somewhere |
| **any-** | anybody / anyone | anything | anywhere |
| **no-** | nobody / no-one | nothing | nowhere |

**Quy tac tuong tu some/any:**
- **Somebody** is waiting for you. (khang dinh)
- Is there **anything** I can do? (cau hoi)
- I told **nobody** about the secret. (phu dinh -- chu y: khong dung "not" truoc "nobody")
- There is **nothing** wrong with your answer.

> **Luu y quan trong:** Khong dung phu dinh kep (double negative):
> - SAI: I don't know **nothing**.
> - DUNG: I don't know **anything**. HOAC: I know **nothing**.

## 3. Much / Many / A lot of

| | Countable (so nhieu) | Uncountable |
|---|---|---|
| **Many** | many students, many books | --- |
| **Much** | --- | much time, much money |
| **A lot of** / **Lots of** | a lot of students | a lot of time |
| **Plenty of** | plenty of ideas | plenty of room |

### Quy tac su dung:

- **Much** it dung trong cau khang dinh. Thay bang **a lot of**:
  - SAI (khong tu nhien): We spent **much** money.
  - DUNG: We spent **a lot of** money.
  - DUNG: We didn't spend **much** money. (phu dinh thi OK)
  - DUNG: Did you spend **much** money? (cau hoi thi OK)

- **Many** dung duoc trong moi loai cau:
  - **Many** people came to the party.
  - I don't have **many** friends here.
  - Do you have **many** books?

- **A lot of** dung trong moi loai cau, voi ca countable va uncountable:
  - She has **a lot of** experience.
  - There are **a lot of** cars on the road.

- **Too much / Too many**: qua nhieu (nghia tieu cuc)
  - You eat **too much** sugar. (khong dem duoc)
  - There are **too many** mistakes in your essay. (dem duoc)

## 4. (A) Few / (A) Little

| | Countable | Uncountable |
|---|---|---|
| **Few** (it, khong du) | few friends | --- |
| **A few** (mot vai, du) | a few friends | --- |
| **Little** (it, khong du) | --- | little time |
| **A little** (mot chut, du) | --- | a little time |

### Phan biet nghia (rat quan trong cho thi!):

- **Few / Little** = it, gan nhu khong co (mang nghia **tieu cuc**):
  - He has **few** friends, so he feels lonely. (it ban, cam thay co don)
  - There is **little** hope of success. (it hy vong)

- **A few / A little** = mot vai, mot chut (mang nghia **tich cuc**, du dung):
  - I have **a few** friends in the city. (co vai nguoi ban, tot thoi)
  - We have **a little** time before the train. (con chut thoi gian, du de uong cafe)

**Vi du so sanh:**
- He spoke **little** English, so communication was difficult. (tieng Anh it qua, kho giao tiep)
- He spoke **a little** English, so we managed to communicate. (biet chut tieng Anh, tam du)

## 5. All / Every / Each

### 5.1. All

- **All** + danh tu so nhieu / khong dem duoc:
  - **All** students must attend. (= tat ca hoc sinh)
  - **All** the information is correct.

- **All of** + the/this/these/my... + danh tu:
  - **All of the** students passed. (= **All the** students passed.)
  - I've read **all of these** books.

- **All of** + us/you/them:
  - **All of us** were happy.

### 5.2. Every

- **Every** + danh tu so it (chia dong tu so it):
  - **Every** student **has** a textbook. (khong phai "have")
  - I go to school **every** day.

### 5.3. Each

- **Each** + danh tu so it (nhung nhan manh tung cai mot):
  - **Each** student received a certificate.
  - **Each of the** rooms has a window.

| All | Every | Each |
|---|---|---|
| Tat ca (nhu mot nhom) | Moi (ca nhom, tong quat) | Moi (tung cai rieng biet) |
| All students | Every student | Each student |
| + plural noun | + singular noun | + singular noun |

## 6. Both / Either / Neither (dung cho HAI doi tuong)

| Tu | Nghia | Vi du |
|---|---|---|
| **Both** | Ca hai | **Both** restaurants are good. |
| **Either** | Bat ky cai nao (trong hai) | You can go to **either** restaurant. |
| **Neither** | Khong cai nao (trong hai) | **Neither** restaurant is expensive. |

### Cau truc voi "of":

- **Both of the** / **both of these** + plural noun:
  - **Both of the** answers are correct.
- **Either of** / **Neither of** + the/these/my... + plural noun (dong tu so it hoac so nhieu):
  - **Neither of them** is/are at home.

### Cap tuong quan:

- **Both ... and ...**: ca ... va ...
  - **Both** Tom **and** Jerry were late.
- **Neither ... nor ...**: khong ... cung khong ...
  - **Neither** Tom **nor** Jerry came to the party.
- **Either ... or ...**: hoac ... hoac ...
  - **Either** you apologize, **or** I'll never speak to you again.

---

## 7. No / None

- **No** + danh tu (dem duoc hoac khong dem duoc):
  - There are **no** buses after midnight.
  - I have **no** money.

- **None** dung mot minh (khong theo sau danh tu):
  - "How much money do you have?" -- "**None**."

- **None of** + the/these/my/them...:
  - **None of the** students failed.
  - **None of them** knew the answer.
  - I invited ten friends, but **none of them** came.

> **Luu y:** Sau "none of + plural noun", dong tu co the o so it hoac so nhieu:
> - **None** of the shops **were** open. (pho bien)
> - **None** of the shops **was** open. (cung dung)

---

## 8. Enough

- **Enough** + danh tu: du (dat truoc danh tu)
  - We have **enough** time. (du thoi gian)
  - There aren't **enough** chairs for everyone.

- Tinh tu / Trang tu + **enough**: du (dat sau adj/adv)
  - She is old **enough** to drive. (khong phai "enough old")
  - He didn't run fast **enough**.

- **Enough** + **for** + somebody + **to do** something:
  - The room is big **enough for** ten people **to sit** comfortably.

## 10. Cac dang de thi thuong gap

### Dang 1: Chon tu dung

**De:** There is ______ milk left. Can you buy some?
- A. a few  B. few  C. a little  D. little
**Dap an:** D. little (it qua, can mua them -- nghia tieu cuc)

**De:** ______ of the two answers is correct.
- A. Both  B. Neither  C. None  D. All
**Dap an:** B. Neither (khong cai nao trong hai)

### Dang 2: Viet lai cau

**De:** "I don't have any free time." -> Rewrite using "no".
**Dap an:** I have **no** free time.

**De:** "Tom was late. Jerry was late too." -> Rewrite using "both".
**Dap an:** **Both** Tom **and** Jerry were late.

### Dang 3: Chia dong tu

**De:** Every student in the class (have) ______ a dictionary.
**Dap an:** has (every + singular noun -> singular verb)

## 11. Loi sai thuong gap

| Sai | Dung | Giai thich |
|---|---|---|
| I don't have **no** money. | I don't have **any** money. | Khong dung double negative |
| She has **much** friends. | She has **many** friends. | Much + uncountable, Many + countable |
| **All of** students passed. | **All (of the)** students passed. | Can "the/these/my" sau "of" |
| He is **enough old** to drive. | He is **old enough** to drive. | Adj + enough (khong phai enough + adj) |
| I have **few** money. | I have **little** money. | Few + countable, Little + uncountable |
| **Every** students must attend. | **Every** student must attend. | Every + singular noun |

## 12. Bai tap tu luyen

**Dien tu thich hop:**

1. There aren't ______ (much / many) students in the class today.
2. I have ______ (a little / a few) questions to ask you.
3. ______ (Both / Neither) of the two roads leads to the city centre.
4. She ate too ______ (much / many) candy and felt sick.
5. ______ (All / Every) the windows were broken.
6. We have ______ (few / little) information about the accident.
7. ______ (None / No) of my friends has been to Japan.
8. Is there ______ (some / any) water in the fridge?

**Dap an:** 1. many  2. a few  3. Both  4. much  5. All  6. little  7. None  8. any
